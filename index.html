What's inside

Everything a note-taker needs

No accounts, no sync, no distractions. Just a clean space for your thoughts.

🗂️

Color-coded Categories

Assign notes to one or multiple categories, each with a custom color. Filter your view instantly with a single click.

🎤

Voice to Text

Speak your thoughts directly into a note. Hit the microphone button and start talking — your words appear automatically.

📌

Pin Important Notes

Pin your most critical notes to the top of the grid so they're always the first thing you see when you open the website.

🗄️

Archive & Trash

Keep your workspace clean by archiving notes you don't need right now. Deleted notes stay in Trash for 3 days before permanent removal.

↩️

Instant Undo

Accidentally deleted something? An undo banner appears for 8 seconds after every delete so you can recover in one click.

🖼️

Image Attachments

Attach a photo or screenshot to any note. Great for saving receipts, sketches, or reference images alongside your text.

🔀

Flexible Sorting

Sort by newest, oldest, title A–Z, last edited, or word count. Your preferred sort is remembered between sessions.

💾

Export & Import

Back up all your notes to a JSON file and restore them anytime. Works as a simple migration tool between browsers too.

🌙

Dark Mode

Toggle between a warm paper-white theme and a deep dark mode. Your preference is saved and restored on every visit.

Getting started

Up and running in minutes

Follow these steps to get the most out of Pen To Text.

1

Create your first note

Click + Add Note in the top bar. Give it a title (optional), write your content, and hit Save. Your note appears instantly in the grid and is automatically saved to your browser's local storage.

Press Ctrl + B anywhere to open a new note immediately.
2

Organise with categories

Use the left sidebar to add categories like Work, Personal, or Ideas. Pick a color for each one. When creating or editing a note, tap a category chip to assign it — notes can belong to multiple categories at once.

Click the ✏️ icon on any category to rename it or change its color at any time.
3

Capture ideas with your voice

Click the red 🎤 microphone button in the header or inside the note editor. Speak clearly and your words will be transcribed directly into the note content field. Works in Chrome, Edge, and Safari.

The mic button turns green and pulses while actively listening. Click it again to stop.
4

Search, sort, and filter

Use the search bar to instantly find notes by title, content, or category tag. Use the sort dropdown to reorder by date, title, last edited, or word count. Click any category in the sidebar to filter the grid to only that category's notes.

5

Back up and restore your notes

Press Ctrl + E to export all your notes to a .json file. To import them back, press Ctrl + I and select your backup file. Notes are merged without duplicates.

Export regularly — local storage can be cleared by the browser.

Power user tips

Keyboard shortcuts

Keep your hands on the keyboard. Every common action has a shortcut.

  • New note Ctrl B
  • Export all notes Ctrl E
  • Import notes Ctrl I
  • Undo last delete Ctrl Z
  • Close modal Esc
